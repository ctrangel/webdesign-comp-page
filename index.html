Webpage Design Competition for Jail Homepage

Create a webpage and upload it to your jail. The best design wins. No guidelines, can be a project, a game, a portfolio, anything goes. This is a good opportunity to add to your resume/portfolio to showcase skills.

Eligibility

  • Must have a jail account.

Submission Guidelines

Submit jail URL to TBD.

Judging Criteria

  • Design and Aesthetics: The visual appeal of the webpage, including layout, color scheme, and graphic elements.
  • Content
  • Usability and Functionality: Navigation ease, loading times, and responsiveness across different devices and browsers.
  • Creativity and Innovation: Originality of the design and innovative use of technology.
  • Technical Implementation: The effectiveness of the underlying code, use of web standards, and best practices.

Prizes

  • First Place: $100 Gift Card
  • Second Place: $50 Gift Card
  • Third Place: $25 Gift Card

Deadlines

Submissions must be received by April 16th. Late submissions will not be considered.

The winners will be announced one week after the Night of Review April 16th, following thorough review and deliberation by our judges, Mark and Jeff.
